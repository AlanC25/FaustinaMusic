Faustina O'Sullivan Soprano & Pianist

Faustina O'Sullivan is a classically trained Soprano and Pianist. She has achieved a Bachelors degree in Music, specialising in Performance from Maynooth University. Currently she studies with the Royal Irish Academy of Music. (RIAM)

Find Out More

Years Of Experience

Along with her studies in Maynooth University, Faustina O'Sullivan has trained/studied with the Royal Irish Academy of Music. She has performed at events and competitions from childhood. Aged 8, she became a semi-finalist on RTE's Star Stream, followed shortly by performances in the chorus section for both Essence(Karen Lynch and Paul Redmond) live on TV3, and Liam Lawlton at the National Concert Hall.

She has since performed in venues such as the National Concert Hall, the Mansion House and the National Gallery of Ireland. She was a member of the Maynooth Ladies Choir from 2013 - 2015, The New Early Music Ensemble of Maynooth in 2015-2016, and the Maynooth Chamber Choir('Choir of the World' winner) in 2017-2018.

She has taught Voice and Piano for nearly a decade, in multiple music schools across Meath & Dublin. She performed at the 'Meath Stars of the Future' showcase. Hosted by Virginia Kerr and the Royal Irish Academy of Music. Most recently, she was accepted into 2 Opera Academies during the summer. The Berlin Opera Academy, where she will perform the role of an Alms Sister in Suor Angelica, and complete a role study as the Dew Fairy in Hansel and Gretel. And, the Lyric Opera Studio Weimar in Germany, where she will be studying the lead role of Barbarina in Le Nozze Di Figaro. (The Marriage of Figaro)
